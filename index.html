What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a digital ledger of transactions, distributed across a network of computers. It is immutable and decentralized, ensuring trust without the need for a central authority.

Key Features

  • Decentralization – No central authority.
  • Security – Encrypted and irreversible transactions.
  • Transparency – Publicly accessible ledger.
  • Immutability – Records can’t be changed or deleted.

Uses of Blockchain

Used in cryptocurrencies, supply chain management, voting systems, healthcare records, and secure financial transactions.
